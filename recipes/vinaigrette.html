Vinaigrette for a Lettuce Salad

Description

A classic vinaigrette dressing for a lettuce salad that my family loves.

I do it differently everytime based on what i find in the cupboard.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Juice from 1/2 of a lemon
  • 2 tbsp of vinegar of your choice (I usually use 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar and 1 tbsp white wine vinegar )
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp silan/honey
  • 1 medium garlic clove
  • Atlantic sea salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. put all the ingredients in a jar
  2. shake jar until mixed
  3. taste and add sugar, salt or pepper if necessery
  4. pour over lettuce salad
  5. Enjoy!
