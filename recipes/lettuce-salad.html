Lettuce Salad

Easy salad recipe that makes a large salad for about 4-6 people as a side dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium heads of lettuce of any kind
  • 1/2 of a red onion
  • A fruit of your choice (I usually use an apple but a pear, some grapes or anything else should work just fine)
  • 1/4 cup halfed almonds (Pinenuts could also work)
  • Dressing of your choice

Steps

  1. Roast the almonds: put them with some salt in an oven that was pre heated to 150°C for 15 minutes, or until browned and crunchy
  2. While the almonds are in the oven, cut lettuce into large chunks
  3. Put the lettuce chunks in a large bowl of water for a few minutes to wash off any dirt
  4. Cut the onion into thin slices, and the apple into medium slices
  5. Dry the lettuce chunks
  6. Mix everything in a large bowl, and add the almonds when they are ready.
  7. Add dressing
  8. Enjoy!
